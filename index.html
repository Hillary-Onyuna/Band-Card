Sauti sol the Band

Sauti Sol is a renowned Kenyan musical band composed of singers, rappers, songwriters, and record producers. The band was formed in Nairobi, Kenya, by vocalists Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Austin Chimano, and Savara Mudigi in 2005. The band’s music mixes Afro-pop, rock, R&B, and electronica. They have released five studio albums: Mwanzo (2008), Sol Filosofia (2011), Live and Die in Africa (2015), Afrikan Sauce (2017), and Midnight Train (2020). Many of Sauti sol songs became very popular and trendy on social media in line with a great number of streaming and downloads on Boomplay.
